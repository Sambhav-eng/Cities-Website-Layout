Lucknow

Lucknow has always been known as a multicultural city that flourished as a North Indian cultural and artistic hub, and the seat of power of Nawabs in the 18th and 19th centuries.[10] It continues to be an important centre of governance, administration, education, commerce, aerospace, finance, pharmaceuticals, technology, design, culture, tourism, music and poetry

Tokyo

Tokyo is Japan's vibrant capital, a vast metropolis on Honshu's Pacific coast known for its dynamic blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. As a global hub, it leads the world in finance, industry, and culture, drawing diverse people and creating new trends.

New York

New York City (NYC) is the most populous city in the U.S., renowned as a global center for finance, culture, and innovation, located on the world's largest natural harbors. Comprised of five distinct boroughs—Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island—it is a melting pot of over 8 million people, speaking more than 800 languages.
